Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Symptom Tracker ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal or health information. All entries you create (symptoms, severity, notes) are stored locally on your device.

2. Backups & Sharing

  • You may choose to export your entries as CSV or encrypted backups.
  • These files are stored only where you save them (e.g., device storage, cloud drive, or email) and under your control.
  • The App never automatically uploads your data to any external server.

3. Advertisements

The free version of the App may display third-party ads. Ad providers may use anonymized data (e.g., device type, general location) to show relevant ads. No personal health data is shared with advertisers.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time purchase to remove ads. Payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. The App does not access your payment information.

5. Permissions

The App may request limited permissions (e.g., file access for backup/restore). These are only used for the stated features and never for hidden data collection.
